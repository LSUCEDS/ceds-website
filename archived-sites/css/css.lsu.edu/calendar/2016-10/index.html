About the studio

CSS brings together disciplines that often work independently such as designers, scientists, engineers, and plannersto respond to critical issues of affecting coastal regions.

Through trans-disciplinary design, research, community engagement, and education, the CSS uses innovative approaches to design and foster resilient coastal communities and ecosystems.
