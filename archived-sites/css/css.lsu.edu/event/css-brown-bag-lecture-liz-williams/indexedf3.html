Liz manages coastal grant-making areas with an advisory committee and relevant partners that work to help communities face a range of issues and challenges that range in scale from mega-regional networks and hydrologic basins to stormwater management and flood insurance.\nRisk mitigation and resilience-based programs require an awareness of and participation with these transitioning watersheds. In this role she led and collaborated with cohorts of civil engineers\, urban planners\, coastal scientists\, and landscape architects alongside economic\, legal\, and cultural advisors. Each project engaged a set of unique conditions within the coastal landscape and proposed developments through which residents and communities might advance and thrive in a future with evolving challenges. \nIn addition to early years of experience within traditional architecture practice\, Liz has held a range of academic positions.
