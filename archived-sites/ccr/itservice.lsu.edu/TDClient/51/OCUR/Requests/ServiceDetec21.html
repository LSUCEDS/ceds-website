    Share a News Story

    What is it?

    Use this form to submit information to be included in the LSU Media Center. If your information is intended for a news release to media, please submit information as early as possible. For more information, contact Ernie Ballard at eballa1@lsu.edu

    NOTE: Submissions made using this form are not submitted to the LSU Events Calendar. To submit an event, please visit https://calendar.lsu.edu/ and login to add your events.

    Who is eligible?

    Users on campus who have news to share with the Communications and University Relations Staff, to the LSU Media Center, or who wish to discuss promoting to media outlets.

    How do I get it?

    Click the "Request Service" button on this page.
