    News and Notes

    What is it?

    Share a story or event with faculty and staff by having it published in the News and Notes e-newsletter. Please note, we will not re-run stories indefinitely and some content may not be the right fit for the newsletter.

    Who is eligible?

    LSU Campus Community.
