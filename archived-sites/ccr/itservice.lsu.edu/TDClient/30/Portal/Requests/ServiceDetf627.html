    Fee Bill Troubleshooting

    What is it?

    Your fee bill includes tuition and fees for the upcoming semester. Paying and submitting your fee bill is also called "completing registration", which is an important term to remember. Completing registration is required to enroll at LSU. Issues with your fee bill may include, but are not limited to: 

    • Error when accessing your fee bill 
    • Unable to submit fee bill/ page never processes 
    • Missing the fee bill option on myLSU

    Who is eligible?

    LSU A&M Students 

    How do I report it?

    Click the "Report Issue" button on this page.
