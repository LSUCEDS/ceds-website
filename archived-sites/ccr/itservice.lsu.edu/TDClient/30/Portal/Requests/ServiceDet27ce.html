    Article Feedback

    Tags knowledge knowledgebase article

    What is it?

    General feedback concerning a specific GROK article or category.

    Who is eligible?

    LSU Faculty, Staff, and Students 

    How do I get it?

    Click the "Request Service" button on this page. Be sure to provide the link to the article or category you are submitting feedback about.

    How do I use it?

    Once form is filled out, GROK team will review and communicate further.
