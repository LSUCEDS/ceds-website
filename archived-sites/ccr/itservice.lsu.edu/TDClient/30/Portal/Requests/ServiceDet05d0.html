    ADA Accessibility Concerns Report

    What is it?

    Please report all accessibility concerns, including physical and digital concerns, using this form. Do not use this form to submit discrimination complaints or grievances. Instead visit our website for discrimination complaints or grievances: https://www.lsu.edu/accessibility/index.php. You may also submit ADA Accessibility concerns to accessibility@lsu.edu. 

    If you are affiliated with LSU and able to sign into the portal in the top right, please do so before submitting this ticket. 

    Who is eligible?

    Anyone

    How do I get it?

    Click the "Request Service" button on this page.
