    Adobe Creative Cloud Ad-Hoc License

    Tags adobe license CC

    What is it?

    Request to provision Adobe Creative Cloud (does not include Adobe Sign) to employees (including Graduate Teaching Assistants or Sponsored Guests) that are primary or secondary instructor of record for a specific course, or providing assistance for a course. The licenses will only be allocated to support instruction and will be addressed on first-come and first-serve basis.

    Who is eligible?

    Faculty and Staff (NOTE: only faculty and staff and request such access for other users, not the users themselves).

    How do I get it?

    Click the "Request Service" button on this page.
